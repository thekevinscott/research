## Summary (2 paragraphs)

The Citrix Blogs piece (Feb 19 2026) is the canonical extrapolation of the dark factory from software to all knowledge work. It treats Shapiro's Level 5 as a leading indicator for executives in audit, banking, legal drafting, and operations — domains where the validation loop is harder but the structural argument identical. Its 18-month obsolescence timeline for current tools, agents, and orchestrations is widely quoted.

Sentiment is enthusiastically forward-looking — Citrix is building product around this thesis, so the framing is naturally optimistic — but the piece is taken seriously by enterprise IT leadership because it ties the dark factory to the company's existing virtual-desktop and workspace-security positioning. It is one of the most-cited "this isn't just about programmers" pieces in the discourse.
