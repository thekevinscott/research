# Summary

Simon Willison's Feb 7 2026 tweet is the most-shared social-media artefact in the dark-factory discourse and the principal pipeline by which the StrongDM announcement reached engineering Twitter. It pairs with his earlier Five Levels promotion (Jan 28) to anchor both Shapiro's framework and StrongDM's existence proof on the same author/voice.

Reply-thread sentiment is bifurcated — enthusiasm vs. domain-fit skepticism — but the tweets unambiguously legitimised the conversation. Dan Shapiro and other commentators amplified, and the link became the canonical share for Slack and group-chat discussions through February-March 2026.
