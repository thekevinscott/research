# Summary

Signals' March 2026 piece is the most-shared synthesis of two findings most other writers treated separately: (1) the dark factory exists at the frontier (StrongDM, Claude Code internal — 90% AI-authored), and (2) most developers using AI tools are measurably slower (the 19% RCT result). The juxtaposition has made this article the standard citation for "the gap is real and not just hype."

The argument that the gap is cultural and organisational rather than technical is widely echoed in subsequent newsletter pieces (Nate's Newsletter, Pragmatic CTO). Sentiment is sharply bifurcated — admiration for frontier teams, frustration at the broad middle — and the article frames the dark factory as both an existence proof and an indictment of how most teams are using AI.
