## Two-paragraph summary

Cognition's Devin blog post is the definitional artifact of the autonomous-software-engineer category and seeds the Dark Factory imagination. It introduced the explicit framing of "fully autonomous AI software engineer," contrasted with copilots, and demonstrated a 13.86% end-to-end SWE-bench resolve rate at launch — an order-of-magnitude jump over prior SOTA.
