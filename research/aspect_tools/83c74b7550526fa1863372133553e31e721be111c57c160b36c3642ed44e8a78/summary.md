# Summary

Cognition's "Introducing Devin" post launched the first commercial fully-autonomous AI software engineer in March 2024. Devin runs in a sandboxed compute environment with a shell, code editor, and browser, and is designed to take a ticket from Linear/Jira/Slack and autonomously deliver a PR through to review iteration. Its initial 13.86% score on SWE-bench was roughly 7x the prior state of the art, establishing the autonomous-engineer category.

In a dark-factory context Devin is one of the canonical "worker" coding agents: it owns the seed-to-merge loop end to end on a per-ticket basis. By 2026 Cognition is at a $10.2B valuation, with Dell and Cisco among customers, demonstrating that the autonomous coding-agent pattern has graduated from demo to enterprise infrastructure.
