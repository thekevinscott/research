## Relevance to dark factories

Aider is the most popular OSS terminal-first coding agent, providing a no-vendor-lock-in worker. In dark factories it commonly serves as a free/local backstop or a script-driven worker in CI pipelines because its commit-by-commit git automation cleanly slots into seed -> validate -> commit loops.
