# Summary

Polyglot_factotum on Medium provides a rare first-hand technical critique of StrongDM's open-sourced Dark Factory artifacts. After reading the released Rust code they call it "a bunch of smoke and mirrors: trying to sound impressive with some pseudo architecture, while in fact just building slop," noting Rust anti-patterns, heavy Arc/Mutex usage and lenient error handling.

They are equally skeptical of the conceptual claims: the Attractor convergence loop is "just an attempt at formal software that isn't formal at all," the Digital Twin Universe seems impractical at scale, and the parts that would matter most - exactly how scenarios are kept as a holdout and how satisfaction is judged - were not released or independently validated. The piece functions as a counterweight to the breathless dark-factory enthusiasm circulating in February 2026.
