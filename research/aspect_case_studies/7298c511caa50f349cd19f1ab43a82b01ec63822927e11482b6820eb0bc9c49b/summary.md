# Summary

Anthony Chaudhary's October 2025 Medium post is one of the earliest first-hand "AI factory" case studies, predating StrongDM's manifesto by several months. He scaled autonomous coding agents over a 30-day experiment that generated 18,000 commits and shipped the backend for a production log-analytics product. What started as an experiment evolved into the team's permanent core production methodology.

The most striking claim is counterintuitive: the AI-factory output exhibits "a level of consistency, standardization, and traceability that is counterintuitively superior to pure human-driven development." The piece is therefore valuable not as a critique but as an early validation of the dark-factory pattern from outside StrongDM - independent evidence that the model works at non-trivial scale and ships real software.
