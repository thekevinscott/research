Cognition's November 2025 Devin annual review is the most extensive primary record of agent deployment across domains. Named customers include Visma (fintech, doubled developer productivity), Nubank (6M+ LOC migration), Goldman Sachs, Santander, EightSleep (3x data features shipped), Litera (40% test coverage gain, 93% faster regressions), and an Oracle Java migration where Devin worked 14x faster than humans on per-repo migration.

The failure modes are domain-agnostic: ambiguous specs, mid-task scope changes, and mentoring/stakeholder work. Devin's success surface is migrations, security-fix backlogs (SonarQube/Veracode), test generation, and well-scoped 4–8 hour junior-engineer tasks — strong evidence that Dark-Factory-adjacent autonomy works in fintech and large-enterprise migration but still gates on human review.
