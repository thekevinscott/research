# Cognition — Devin Review: AI to Stop Slop

Cognition's product post on Devin Review: an autonomous code-review counterpart to the Devin coding agent. Designed to catch "AI slop" — low-quality output from other AI agents — before merge. The framing acknowledges that Dark Factory operation creates a slop problem at scale, and that the solution is more agents, not fewer, with explicit reviewer roles.

Important because it surfaces the validator/generator split as a product category, not just a paper concept. Devin Review is sold as the second half of an autonomous loop where Devin codes and Devin Review checks. Cognition argues this is one of the few configurations where Level-5 operation is safe — generation and validation isolated, both autonomous, both audited.
