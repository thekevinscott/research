# Cognition — Devin's 2025 Performance Review

Cognition's annual review reports concrete production metrics for Devin: 67% PR merge rate (vs 34% the prior year), 4x faster problem-solving, 2x more efficient resource use. Adopters include Goldman Sachs, Santander, and Nubank; specific case studies note 5–10% of total developer time saved at one large org by routing security fixes to Devin (1.5 minutes per vulnerability vs 30 minutes for humans).

The review acknowledges Devin's limits: senior at codebase understanding but junior at execution, and only 15% of complex tasks completed without human help. Cognition launched Devin 2.0 at $20/month to make the agent broadly accessible. This is one of the few primary Level-4/5 case-study posts grounded in shipped numbers from a vendor with 18 months of production telemetry.
