A Q1-2026 assessment of 200+ vibe-coded applications found 91.5% contained at least one vulnerability traceable to AI hallucination. This is the population-level evidence for a Dark Factory failure mode: throughput goes up, vulnerability density goes up faster, and review is the thing being eliminated. The trade is real, not theoretical.
