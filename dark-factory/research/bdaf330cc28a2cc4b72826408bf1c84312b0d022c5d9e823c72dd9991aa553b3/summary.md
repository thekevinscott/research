# Summary — Feng et al — AgentRR: LLM Agents with Record & Replay

Record-and-replay framework for LLM agents that captures interaction traces and decision processes, summarizes them into multi-level experience abstractions, and replays with check functions as safety validation.

This source is part of the validation aspect (Step 3) of the Dark Factory research. It bears on how teams that operate at Shapiro Level 5 (no human reviews code) prove correctness. Source tier: 1 (primary). Recency: 2025. Search term: 'deterministic replay harness'. URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.17716.
